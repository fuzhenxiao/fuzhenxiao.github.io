Zhenxiao Fu

Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Intelligent Systems Engineering at Indiana University Bloomington, where I am on the Computer Engineering track. Prior to joining Indiana University, I received my M.S. degree in Electronic Engineering and my B.S. degree in Physics from ShanghaiTech University.

Research / Project Interests

My current Ph.D. research focuses on LLM reasoning and tool-augmented LLM agent systems, with particular interest in extending LLM-based methods to quantum computing and quantum security. I am also involved in projects that explore the use of LLMs in other domains requiring substantial analysis and complex reasoning.

I am also interested in the inference-time performance of LLMs, especially their latency and energy characteristics, as well as the energy behavior of deploying LLMs on edge devices.

During my master's study, my research focused on the modeling and numerical simulation of spintronic systems, together with FPGA and circuit design for spintronic devices.
